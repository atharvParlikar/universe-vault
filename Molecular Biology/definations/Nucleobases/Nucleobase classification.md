
Nucleobases are classified into two types:

- **Purines**: Adenine and Guanine.
- **Pyrimidines**: Thymine, Cytosine and Uracil.

### Pairs

- Adenine == Thymine (in DNA)
- Adenine == Uracil (in RNA)
- Guanine == Cytosine
